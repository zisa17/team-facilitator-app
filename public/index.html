Why retrospectives matter
📈 Teams that run regular retros improve 40% faster than those that don't, according to research on high-performing agile teams.
🔁 A retro isn't a complaint session — it's a structured learning loop. What worked? What didn't? What will we try next?
🎯 The most important part is the action items. A retro without committed actions is just a conversation.
⏱️ Keep retros time-boxed. 45-60 minutes is enough for most teams. Use the built-in timer to stay on track.
